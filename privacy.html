Music Playground Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Music Playground respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Music Playground mobile application.

Music Playground is designed as an educational music learning app for children and beginners.

1. Information We Collect

Music Playground does not collect personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Home addresses
  • Photos or contacts

The app does not require user accounts or registration.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Some limited technical information may be automatically collected by the operating system or app distribution platform. This may include:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash or diagnostic data

This information helps improve the stability and performance of the app.

3. Device Permissions

Music Playground may request access to certain device features in order to provide educational functionality.

Microphone

The microphone may be used for interactive music learning activities such as rhythm or pitch exercises.

Audio input is processed locally on the device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Audio Playback

The app uses your device’s audio system to play music examples, instruments, and educational demonstrations.

4. In-App Purchases

Music Playground may offer optional content through in-app purchases.

All purchases are securely processed through Apple and the App Store.

The developer does not collect or store payment information. Payment data is handled entirely by Apple according to Apple’s privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Music Playground is designed for children and families.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that personal information has been inadvertently collected, please contact us so it can be promptly removed.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise children's use of mobile applications.

6. Third-Party Services

Music Playground relies on services provided by Apple, including:

  • App distribution through the App Store
  • In-app purchase processing
  • Optional diagnostic and crash reporting tools provided by Apple

These services may collect limited technical data in accordance with Apple’s privacy policies.

Music Playground does not include third-party advertising networks.

7. Data Security

Music Playground is designed to minimise the collection of user data. Because the app does not collect personal information, risks to user privacy are limited.

Reasonable technical measures are used to ensure the app operates securely.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect updates to the app, new features, or changes in legal requirements.

The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised update date.
